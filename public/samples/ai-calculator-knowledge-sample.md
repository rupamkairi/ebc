# EBC Sample Knowledge Pack: Single Room Costing (500 sq ft)

## Scope
This sample is for a single-floor room of approximately 500 sq ft carpet area in an urban Indian location.
The estimate is for planning only and excludes statutory fees and unforeseen site risks.

## Structural Assumptions
- Foundation: isolated footing on medium soil bearing capacity.
- Wall type: brick masonry, 9-inch external and 4.5-inch internal partitions.
- Roof: RCC slab with basic waterproofing.
- Floor to ceiling height: 10 feet.

## Mix Ratios and Curing
- RCC slab concrete mix (nominal): 1:1.5:3 (cement:sand:aggregate).
- Brick mortar mix: 1:6 (cement:sand).
- Internal plaster mix: 1:5 (cement:sand).
- External plaster mix: 1:4 (cement:sand).
- Minimum slab curing duration: 14 days.
- Preferred slab curing duration for durability: 21 days.

## Cost Assumptions (Indicative)
- Foundation and plinth: INR 2,80,000
- Wall masonry and lintels: INR 2,25,000
- RCC slab and waterproofing: INR 2,40,000
- Flooring and skirting: INR 1,55,000
- Door and window package (1 main door, 1 window): INR 85,000
- Electrical and plumbing rough-in: INR 1,15,000
- Paint and finishing: INR 1,05,000
- Labor overhead and supervision: INR 1,45,000

Estimated subtotal: INR 13,50,000
Recommended contingency (8%): INR 1,08,000
Estimated project total: INR 14,58,000

## Quality Checklist Before Final Paint
1. Verify plaster is cured and surface moisture is controlled.
2. Ensure crack-filling and putty are complete and fully dry.
3. Confirm electrical switch boxes are aligned and covered.
4. Sand all patched surfaces for uniform finish.
5. Apply primer coat and inspect under daylight before final coats.

## Exclusions
- Government approvals, permit fees, and legal charges.
- Major soil correction, rock cutting, or flood mitigation works.
- Premium branded fixtures beyond baseline assumptions.
- Furniture, modular kitchen, and home automation.

## Risk Notes
- Cement and steel price volatility can shift costs by +/-7%.
- Labor availability can delay schedules by 2 to 4 weeks.
- Transport costs vary significantly by district.
